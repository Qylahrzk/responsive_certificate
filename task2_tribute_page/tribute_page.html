Tun Dr. Mahathir Mohamad

Tun Dr. Mahathir delivering a speech during his second term as Prime Minister of Malaysia.

Tun Dr. Mahathir Mohamad is a prominent Malaysian politician and former Prime Minister who served twice, from 1981–2003 and 2018–2020. Known for his strong leadership and modernisation of Malaysia’s economy, Mahathir is one of the most influential figures in Southeast Asian history.
